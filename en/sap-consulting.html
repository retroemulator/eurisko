Eurisko / SAP Consulting

Consulting services for every project phase.

From the initial setup to final support, we walk alongside our clients through the entire SAP project lifecycle — with deep product knowledge and a process-oriented approach.

01 · Strategy

Program Management & ICT Governance

We run complex transformation programs with proven methodologies: planning, ownership, risk management and measurable KPIs from kick-off to go-live.

Our approach integrates project governance and technical governance, making sure IT decisions stay aligned with the client's business objectives.

Project planning Risk management Stakeholder alignment KPI tracking Change management
02 · Processes

Business Process Re-engineering

We analyze existing processes (as-is) and redesign them (to-be) to fully leverage SAP standard solutions, cutting unnecessary customizations and maintenance costs.

We always start from real data: interviews with key users, process mapping, pain-point analysis. Only then do we design the solution.

Process mapping Gap analysis Fit-to-standard SAP best practices Blueprint design
03 · Technology

Systems Integration

We integrate SAP with the rest of the IT ecosystem — MES, CRM, PLM, legacy systems — guaranteeing data consistency, real-time sync and operational continuity.

We know the SAP integration technologies (PI/PO, CPI, IDOC, BAPI, web services) and we know when to use each one. We work side by side with the client's IT teams.

SAP PI/PO SAP CPI IDOC REST/SOAP Master data sync Custom ABAP
