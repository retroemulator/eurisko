What we do, in practice.

Three areas of expertise, eight SAP modules, nine industries. Twenty years of projects. This is the map of everything we can do for you.

01 · SAP Consulting

End-to-end services, from kick-off to go-live.

Program management, business process re-engineering, systems integration. We support the client at every stage, with proven methodologies and a process-oriented approach.

01 · Strategy

Program Management & ICT Governance

We manage complex transformation programs with planning, ownership, risk management and measurable KPIs. Project governance and technical governance always aligned with business objectives.

02 · Processes

Business Process Re-engineering

We analyse as-is processes and redesign them to-be to leverage SAP standard solutions. User interviews, flow mapping, pain-point analysis: the solution comes after, not before.

03 · Technology

Systems Integration

We integrate SAP with MES, CRM, PLM, legacy systems. We know SAP integration technologies (PI/PO, CPI, IDOC, BAPI, web services) and when to use them.
