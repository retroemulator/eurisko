03 · S/4HANA migration

SAP ECC goes out of support in 2027.

Three years seem like a lot but they aren't. Greenfield, Brownfield or Selective Data Transition: we choose the right strategy together with the client, assessing scope, risk, timing, cost.

01

Greenfield

Implementation from scratch on S/4HANA, leveraging the most modern SAP standard processes. Ideal when the current system carries a lot of obsolete customizations, or when the business has changed significantly.

02

Brownfield

Technical conversion of the existing system, preserving configurations, data and customizations. A faster approach, ideal when the current system is "clean" and processes don't need redesign.

03

Selective Data Transition

A hybrid approach: choose which data and processes to bring into S/4HANA and what to redesign. The right balance for complex multi-company or multi-country organizations.
