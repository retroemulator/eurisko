What's included

Run, optimize, evolve.

Eurisko AMS isn't simple "maintenance": it's a continuous partnership that keeps the system stable, aligned with SAP releases and constantly evolving with the business.

01

Run

Operational management: multi-level service desk, H24 monitoring, incident and problem management. Response and recovery times certified by SLA, structured escalations, monthly reporting.

02

Optimize

Adaptive maintenance: OSS notes, patching, performance fine-tuning, governance of integrations. The system stays aligned with SAP best practices and updates.

03

Evolve

New feature development, process evolution, support to the S/4HANA roadmap. AMS is the engine that lets your SAP keep growing with the business: small interventions that prevent big projects.

How it works

Onboarding, run, improve.

  1. 01

    Onboarding

    Knowledge transfer from the project team, baseline analysis, mapping of customizations and integrations.

  2. 02

    Setup

    Service desk configuration, operational runbooks, SLA definition, monitoring plan and escalation channels.

  3. 03

    Run

    H24 service delivery with ticket pickup, corrective, adaptive and improvement maintenance.

  4. 04

    Reporting

    Monthly KPIs, quarterly service reviews, shared governance with the client's IT team.

  5. 05

    Continuous improvement

    Evolutionary roadmap: new development requests, optimizations, support for transformation initiatives (Fiori, BTP, S/4HANA).
