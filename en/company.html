Eurisko / Our Vision

Welcome to the world of Eurisko.

We are an organizational and technology consulting firm — the ideal partner for those who want to upgrade their information systems to the new standards required by the market, with a practical approach and a proactive mindset. Since 2007 we've been working alongside Italian companies in the transformation of their SAP processes. Almost twenty years of projects, mistakes, successes, evolution — built one client at a time, with the right people at the right moment.

Our values

Principles that guide every project.

Three pillars that orient our daily work and our relationships with clients.

Vision

We anticipate signals of change coming from the market and react quickly, walking alongside clients in every need and opportunity for development.

Goal

We deliver what clients need to run the core of their business, proposing tailored solutions grounded in real business processes.

Method

Through professionals with strong motivation and deep product knowledge, we guide the client by defining together the most efficient path to the goal.

Our Values

Four promises, one way of working.

The four principles we commit to with every client, project and team member.

01

We are agile and innovative

We adapt quickly to change and bring pragmatic innovation to every SAP project.

02

03
