Selection process

How it works.

Transparent, fast, no surprises. From application to offer in 2 to 4 weeks on average.

  1. 01

    Application

    Fill in the form below and attach your CV. You'll receive a confirmation of receipt within 24 hours.

  2. 02

    CV screening

    Our HR team reviews the profile. We get back to you (positive or negative) within 5 business days.

  3. 03

    HR interview

    A 45-minute conversation about your experience, motivation and expectations. Online or on-site.

  4. 04

    Technical interview

    Deep dive with a senior consultant from your area of interest. Real scenarios drawn from our projects.

  5. 05

    Offer

    If the match works both ways, we make an offer within 7 days from the technical interview. We discuss salary, role and growth path openly.

Internships and graduates

03

Thesis with the company

Develop your bachelor's or master's thesis on a real-world SAP case, with a dedicated academic-corporate tutor.
