SAP HR / HCM

Human resources: employee master data, org chart, time management and payroll integration.

Overview

What the module covers.

The SAP HCM (Human Capital Management) area covers the full employee lifecycle: master data, organisational structure, time and attendance, integration with third-party or SAP Payroll. For cloud projects, SAP SuccessFactors is available.

When it matters

Typical scenarios.

  • Multi-agreement and multi-site workforce management
  • Time management with automatic badge-based capture
  • Integration with third-party Italian payroll (Zucchetti, Inaz, etc.)
  • Progressive migration to SAP SuccessFactors (hybrid HR)
What we do

Our services on HR / HCM.

  • PA (Personnel Administration) and OM (Organizational Management) configuration
  • Time management schemas and rules for Italian labour agreements
  • Interfaces with third-party payroll (infotypes, flat-file, IDoc)
  • Employee Central SuccessFactors ↔ on-premise ERP integration
  • Fiori employee and manager self-service
Integration

How it connects with the rest.

Integrated with FI (personnel cost posting), CO (allocations), PM (technician assignment), PP (operator capacity), external payroll providers.
