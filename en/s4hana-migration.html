Why start now

Three years feel like a lot, but they're not.

Migrating to S/4HANA isn't a technical upgrade: it's an opportunity to rethink processes, cut technical debt and open the way to SAP Fiori, embedded analytics and AI.

01

Greenfield

Implementation from scratch on S/4HANA, leveraging the most modern SAP standard processes. Ideal when the current system carries a lot of obsolete customizations, or when the business has changed significantly.

02

Brownfield

Technical conversion of the existing system, preserving configurations, data and customizations. A faster approach, ideal when the current system is "clean" and processes don't need redesign.

03

Selective Data Transition

A hybrid approach: choose which data and processes to bring into S/4HANA and what to redesign. The right balance for complex multi-company or multi-country organizations.

Our approach

Discovery, decision, delivery.

  1. 01

    Assessment

    Technical and functional analysis of the current system, mapping of customizations and technical debt.

  2. 02

    Strategy

    Choose the approach (Greenfield, Brownfield, SDT) with business case and high-level roadmap.

  3. 03

    Blueprint

    Detailed design of to-be processes, gap analysis against the S/4HANA standard.

  4. 04

    Migration

    Conversion or re-implementation, data migration, end-to-end testing, user training.

  5. 05

    Hypercare & Run

    Post go-live support, optimization and evolution roadmap (Fiori, BTP, Analytics).
