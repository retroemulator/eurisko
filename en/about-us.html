Eurisko / About us

A story about people.

Since 2007 we've been working alongside Italian companies in the transformation of their SAP processes. Almost twenty years of projects, mistakes, successes, evolution — built one client at a time, with the right people at the right moment.

Our story

Since 2007, one step at a time.

A consulting firm founded in Piedmont and grown alongside Italian companies on their digital transformation journey.

2007

The founding

Eurisko S.r.l. is born in Cascinette d'Ivrea with a clear idea: deliver high-quality SAP consulting to Italian companies, with a process-oriented approach and an "outside the box" mindset. First clients in the Piedmontese automotive sector.

2010—2014

Growth by industry

Vertical expansion: from manufacturing to food & beverage, from utilities to aerospace. Deep knowledge of real client processes becomes our trademark. The team grows with senior consultants and business analysts with industry-specific experience.

2015—2020

International roll-outs

Eurisko supports Italian clients on their SAP projects abroad — over 40 countries reached. Specialisation grows on systems integration (PI/PO, CPI, IDOC) and on complex multi-country scenarios. The process-driven delivery model takes shape.

2021—2025

The S/4HANA era

With SAP ECC support ending in 2027, Eurisko positions itself as a partner for S/4HANA migrations — Greenfield, Brownfield, Selective Data Transition. The portfolio surpasses 30 Top Tier clients and 1500+ delivered projects.

Today

Looking forward

Eurisko keeps investing in people — recruitment, ongoing training, mentorship — and in technology evolution: generative AI applied to SAP processes, automation, digital sustainability.
