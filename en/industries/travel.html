Solutions / Travel

Travel.

Shipping, logistics and cruise companies run capital-intensive fleets, 24/7 operations and volatile margins. SAP is the tool to govern assets, revenue and sustainability.

Industry challenges

Fleet, revenue, sustainability.

Three areas where SAP plays a critical role in travel.

01

Fleet & asset management

Predictive maintenance, dry-docking plans, onboard documentation, asset tracking and certifications.

02

Revenue & dynamic pricing

Dynamic pricing models, yield management, demand forecasting and unit revenue optimization by route or cabin.

03

Sustainability & ESG

Emission reduction, alternative fuels, ESG reporting and compliance with international maritime regulations.

How we support the industry

SAP for those who move people and goods.

Four areas where we bring experience on travel and shipping projects.

  1. 01

    Asset Management & PM

    Predictive maintenance, fleet lifecycle, maintenance orders and onboard spare parts.

  2. 02

    Multi-entity finance

    Consolidation for multi-company shipping groups, multi-currency, complex intercompany.

  3. 03

    Travel & Expense

    Onboard procurement, crew expense management, expense reports and reimbursements.

  4. 04

    Analytics & ESG reporting

    Operational dashboards, ESG reporting on emissions and sustainability, regulatory compliance.
