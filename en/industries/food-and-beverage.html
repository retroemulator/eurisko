Solutions / Food & Beverage

Food & Beverage.

Food & beverage runs on speed, full traceability and supply chains that respond to seasonality and promotions. SAP is the nervous system of the industry, from raw material to shelf.

Industry challenges

From field to shelf.

Three priorities that shape SAP projects in food and beverage.

01

Traceability & food safety

Supply chain traceability from field to consumer, allergen management, fast and selective recall capability.

02

Shelf-life, seasonality, promotions

Demand planning with seasonal variables, shelf-life management and promotion orchestration within short windows.

03

Retail-ready supply chain

Serving retail, HoReCa and export from one ERP: high service levels, EDI with distributors, multi-currency export.

How we support the industry

SAP for consumer goods.

Four key areas where we bring proven experience to food & beverage projects.

  1. 01

    Batch & lot management

    End-to-end traceability of batches and raw materials, expiration management, selective recalls.

  2. 02

    Demand & supply planning

    Forecasting with seasonality, promotion planning, collaborative planning with distribution.

  3. 03

    Trade Promotion Management

    Retail promo planning, trade contract management, accruals and ROI analysis on promo spend.

  4. 04

    EDI & retail integration

    EDI exchanges with retail chains, interfaces with logistics partners' WMS, multi-channel order management.
