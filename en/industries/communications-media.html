Solutions / Communications & Media

Communications & Media.

Publishing, TV and telco converge in the challenge of monetizing digital audiences, managing rights and content, scaling subscriptions. SAP is the stack behind advertising, subscriptions and digital revenue.

Industry challenges

Value is moving online.

Three critical areas for those selling content, audience and advertising.

01

Subscription & digital monetization

Digital subscriptions, paywalls, bundles, freemium: models that require flexible billing and precisely managed customer lifecycles.

02

Rights & royalties management

Licenses, royalties, territorial rights and release windows: contractual tracking and fair redistribution of revenue to rights holders.

03

Advertising & revenue recognition

Multi-channel ad sales, performance campaigns, revenue recognition compliant with international accounting standards.

How we support the industry
