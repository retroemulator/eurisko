Solutions / Utilities

Utilities.

Utilities run capital-intensive assets and massive customer volumes. Between energy transition, smart meters and complex billing, SAP is the backbone of the industry.

Industry challenges

Networks, customers, transition.

Three themes that shape SAP projects in utilities.

01

Energy transition

Renewables, decarbonization, new distributed generation models and managing an increasingly complex energy mix.

02

Customer service & mass billing

Millions of customers, periodic invoicing, collections, multichannel customer care and evolving industry regulations.

03

Network assets & smart meters

Distributed network infrastructure, preventive maintenance, smart metering and real-time consumption data management.

How we support the industry

SAP for utilities.

Four key capabilities we bring to utilities projects.

  1. 01

    SAP IS-U

    Industry Solution Utilities: customer master data, supply contracts, customer lifecycle, device management.

  2. 02

    FI-CA (Contract Accounting)

    Customer accounting at mass volume, periodic billing, collections and arrears management.

  3. 03

    Plant Maintenance for networks

    Distributed network asset management, preventive maintenance plans, work orders and field intervention management.

  4. 04

    Analytics & smart metering

    Smart meter data processing, consumption analytics, demand forecasting and operational dashboards.
