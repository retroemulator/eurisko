Solutions / Chemicals

Chemicals.

The chemical industry runs on safety, compliance and precise batch management. SAP is the backbone that governs recipes, regulations and complex supply chains.

Industry challenges

Compliance, batch, sustainability.

Three themes that shape SAP deployments in the chemical industry.

01

Regulatory compliance

REACH, CLP, GMP, Dangerous Goods: documentation, safety data sheets, classification and audit.

02

Batch & lot management

End-to-end traceability of batches and raw materials, certificates of analysis, product recalls.

03

Production sustainability

Lower consumption and emissions, circular economy, ESG reporting increasingly demanded by customers and regulators.

How we support the industry

SAP for the process industry.

Four capabilities we bring to SAP projects in chemicals and process manufacturing.

  1. 01

    PP-PI (Process Industry)

    Recipe management, process orders, reactor planning and production campaigns.

  2. 02

    Quality Management

    Quality plans, certificates of analysis, batch release and non-conformance management.

  3. 03

    EH&S

    Environment, Health & Safety: safety data sheets, substance classification, waste management.

  4. 04

    Extended Warehouse

    EWM for chemical warehouses: product segregation, compatible storage, advanced traceability.
