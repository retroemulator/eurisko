Eurisko / Our Vision / Inclusion & Diversity

Inclusion & Diversity.

For us these aren't slogans: they're operational criteria we apply every time we make decisions about people — recruitment, compensation, training, promotions. Meritocracy isn't a tagline, it's a method.

Our principles

Three rules, non-negotiable.

The guidelines that run through every decision we make about the people at Eurisko.

01

Equal opportunity, no exceptions

Age, gender, sexual orientation, ethnicity, religion, political opinions, disability, marital status: none of these dimensions affect the decisions we make about our people.

02

Transparent meritocracy

Compensation, growth paths and responsibilities are guided by objective criteria: skills, contribution to the team, results. Paths are clear, communicated and verifiable.

03

Zero tolerance

Harassment, discrimination, retaliation: not tolerated. We have reporting channels and a structured procedure to handle any such episode confidentially and promptly.

How we apply it

Four concrete areas.

From principles to practice: where and how our Inclusion & Diversity policy translates into operational choices.

  1. 01

    Recruitment

    Neutral job descriptions, diverse interview panels, evaluation criteria defined before the meeting to reduce bias.

  2. 02

    Compensation and promotions

    Transparent pay bands, structured annual review, separation between skill assessment and subjective judgment.

  3. 03

    Training

    Equal access to SAP training and certification opportunities, regardless of seniority, role or assignment area.

  4. 04

    Reporting

    Dedicated channel — anonymous if needed — to report behaviors not aligned with the code of ethics. Every report is reviewed.

Think outside the box and treat others with the same respect you'd expect to receive.
